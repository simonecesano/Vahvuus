Bookmarklet
-----------

Our bookmarklet allows you to quickly add a point of interest to your current map from Google maps. 

It's simple:

- drag and drop the
<a href='javascript:(function(){var%20w=window.open("");%20w.location.href="http://oxiana.herokuapp.com/poi/add?url="+unescape(encodeURIComponent(window.location.href))})()'>Add to Vahvuus</a> bookmarklet to your toolbar 
- search for a place on Google maps
- make sure that you choose only one of the results
- click on the bookmarklet on your toolbar
- done!
